    Chinese Cultural Festival

    PCA participated in the yearly Chinese Cultural Festival held at Mellon Park with activities like making bookmarks and face painting. The festival included Asian cuisine, traditional performances, fun games, and more.

    Date: Saturday, September 16, 11:00 AM - 6:00 PM
    Location: 6518 Fifth Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

    我们很高兴宣布PCA会参加2023年的匹兹堡中华文化节！我们会有很多的活动，比如做书签和面部彩绘。中华文化节会有亚洲口味的美食，正宗的表演，和很多好玩的游戏！

    Summer Field Day

    PCA Youth Center Student Council hosted a fun field day at North Park. Stations included relays, tic-tac-toe, cup pong, themed races, and large group games such as capture the flag, sharks/minnows, water fights, tug of war, limbo, and more.

    The field day was hosted at the North Star Shelter on June 18 from 2-5 PM.

    Design Seminar

    Chris Ference, a designer and experimenter based in Rochester, New York, hosted a talk called "The New Wave of Designers." Participants learned about speculative design, discovering passions, pursuing creative careers, curiosity and observation, and networking with professionals.

    Coding Workshops

    PCA Youth Center held a Coding Workshop split into two groups: a Scratch coding workshop for beginners using block coding and a Discord bot tutorial for more experienced coders using JavaScript. Each group had time to work on an individual project.

    Lunar New Year Celebration

    The PCA Student Council hosted a Lunar New Year celebration over Zoom with Chinese paper cutting, origami, cooking, and dancing.

    Date: February 14th, 2021

    • 2:00 PM Dancing: favorite dances, singing, and fun activities for grades 5 and under.
    • 3:00 PM Origami: participants folded an Ox Head to celebrate the Year of the Ox.
    • 3:45 PM Paper Cutting: participants folded and cut an Ox head out of paper.
    • 4:30 PM Cooking: participants learned about Chinese culture and authentic Chinese dishes. PCA planned to create a cookbook from family submissions.

    Shining Through the Holidays

    To end 2020, PCA Student Council introduced Shining Through the Holidays. Participants shared pictures of holiday decorations, including DIY decorations, festive rooms, or brightly lit yards. Winners were selected by creativity, popularity, and overall festivity.

    Submission Period: 12/11/20 - 1/3/21

    大家好！不同寻常的2020已经进入倒计时了！相信年底的假期也会不同以往，多数人将选择继续宅家。PCA学生会为丰富群友的假期生活，精心策划推出“庆圣诞，迎新年之节日装饰大赛”！

    Photography Show

    Submissions for the photography show were open until the end of November 2020. The four themes were light, holidays, animals, and nature. Judges reviewed pictures using creativity; color, lighting, exposure, and focus; composition; and clarity and quality.

    SnapShot!

    SnapShot was an online seminar about street photography, also known as candid photography. Participants learned to capture color to enhance their shots.

    Date: Saturday, November 7th
    Time: 2 PM

    Meet the Speaker: Dr. Min Sun

    Dr. Sun is an oncologist at UPMC Hillman Cancer Center and a senior photography enthusiast. He started shooting at the age of eight and enjoys capturing sports, wild animals, nature, people, weddings, and street photography.
