Membership

PCA Youth Center membership is open to anyone in the Greater Pittsburgh area.

Membership is based on the fiscal year, July 1 through June 30 of the following year, with renewals occurring in June.

For a limited time only: if joining the PCA Youth Center now, you will receive additional months for free with a paid membership. Your membership will be effective March 1, 2025, and good through June 30, 2026.

Annual membership fee: $25 per household, including all adults and children living at the address. The money will be used only for PCA Youth Center events, prizes, and operating costs.

Member Benefits and Rights

Discounted Event Fees

Members receive a 50% discount on any paid events organized by PCA Youth Center.

Priority Registration

Members are notified first about PCA Youth Center volunteer event signups. Volunteer opportunities will be given to members first.

Voting Rights

Members may vote in board elections. Each household has one vote.
