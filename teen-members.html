Learn, Lead, and Serve

Teen Members

One approved Teen Member Account can hold more than one role. Administrators review every application and assign roles that match each teen's interests.

Student Council

Join the authorization-ready council roster and build leadership skills while helping plan PCA programs.

Blog Editor

Write, edit, and publish your own structured articles through PCA's clean blog editor.

Volunteer

Receive event assignments, share your availability, and submit service hours for administrator review.

How approval works

  1. Create a Teen Member Account using the teen's own email address.
  2. Submit basic identity and guardian-consent information.
  3. An administrator approves the application and assigns one or more roles.
  4. If Volunteer is assigned, complete the additional volunteer profile from the dashboard.

  • Open Teen Dashboard

Volunteer service

Teen Members with the Volunteer role can help with event setup and tear down, proctoring, cultural programs, and community service projects. PCA Youth Center remains a certifying organization for the President's Volunteer Service Award; program availability is announced by PCA.
