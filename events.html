    PCA Programs

    Events

    In general, we have new events every month that are widely based on public interest and educational topics.

    • Upcoming Events
    • Past Events

    Community

    The goal of these volunteer activities is mainly to help our community's people and environment. Some past events include tree planting, food bank distribution with Jubilee Kitchen, and donation drives.

    Cultural

    We host cultural events for Chinese New Year and the Mid-Autumn Festival that include activities such as lantern-making, origami, and outdoor games. We also often have mooncakes, bookmarks, and shirts for sale.

    Workshops

    Our workshops are geared toward youth to help them learn about educational and entertaining topics, including coding, speech and debate, business, social etiquette, and more.
