Community Service

Volunteer

Volunteering at PCA Youth Center includes helping with event setup and tear down, proctoring events when necessary, and supporting community service projects.

Volunteer photo placeholder

Volunteering in the PCA Youth Center entails helping with setup and tear down at events as well as helping with proctoring certain events when necessary. This includes everyone in the Student Council as well as those in the Volunteer Group.

Student Council

Joining the Student Council opens you to a new network of opportunities. Attend weekly meetings and collaborate with others to plan events. Develop leadership and communication skills while giving back to the community.

You can apply to the Student Council during our annual recruitment in July. We will announce here when the recruitment period starts.

Volunteer Group

The Volunteer Group provides many opportunities and experiences to anyone who applies. Jobs include event proctoring, setup, and tear down.

President's Volunteer Service Award

PCA Youth Center is a certifying organization for the President's Volunteer Service Award. We provide certifications for anyone who has volunteered at the PCA Youth Center with at least 25 hours in the certified year.

If you volunteered at the PCA Youth Center in 2024 and want to apply for the 2024 award, please send the following to pcayouthcenter@gmail.com by Friday 3/28/2025.

  • Fill out the volunteer time tracking form.
  • If you would like to include volunteer hours served at other organizations, please send proof that documents the number of hours with proper signatures and supervisor contact information so we can contact them for verification.
