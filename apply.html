PCA Youth Center is excited to announce that we will be accepting new Student Council applicants for the 2024-2025 school year. Applicants must be going into 7th grade or above in the next school year. The application form will close on July 14th, 2024 at 10:00 PM. Results will be revealed by July 28th, 2024, and for those who are accepted, there will be an introduction meeting on August 18th, 2024.
